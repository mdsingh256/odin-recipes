Grilled Cheese

Odin Recipes

Description:

A grilled cheese sandwich is a classic comfort food made by grilling two slices of bread with melted cheese between them until the bread is crispy and golden brown and the cheese is hot and gooey. It's simple, delicious, and often paired with a bowl of tomato soup for a perfect meal.

Ingredients:

  • White Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Bread: Spread butter evenly on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich: Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in a skillet or frying pan. Add slices of cheese on top, then place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side up.
  3. Cook the Sandwich: Heat the skillet over medium heat. Cook the sandwich until the bread is golden brown and crispy, and the cheese is melted, about 2-3 minutes per side. Flip carefully halfway through cooking.
